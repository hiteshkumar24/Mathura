Traditional Dress

Mathura's traditional dress is colorful and vibrant, reflecting the rich cultural heritage. Women typically wear sarees adorned with intricate designs, while men wear dhotis and kurtas. During festivals, the traditional attire becomes even more elaborate and beautiful.

Festivals

Mathura is famous for its grand celebrations of festivals like Janmashtami and Holi. During Janmashtami, which celebrates the birth of Lord Krishna, the city comes alive with decorations, dance performances (Raslila), and religious processions. Holi, the festival of colors, is celebrated with unmatched enthusiasm, with people smearing each other with colors and singing traditional songs.
